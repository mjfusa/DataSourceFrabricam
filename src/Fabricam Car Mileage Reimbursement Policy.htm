Fabricam Car Mileage Reimbursement Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2024

1. Overview

This document outlines the car mileage reimbursement policy for Fabricam employees. The policy is designed to compensate employees for the use of their personal vehicles for business purposes in a fair and equitable manner.

2. Eligibility

Employees eligible for mileage reimbursement must have prior approval from their immediate supervisor or manager. Reimbursement is only applicable for business-related travel and does not cover commuting to and from the employee's usual place of work.

3. Reimbursement Rate

The reimbursement rate is set at $0.58 per mile. This rate is subject to annual review and adjustments based on the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) guidelines.

4. Claim Submission

·       Employees must submit a mileage reimbursement form within 30 days of the travel date.

·       The form must include the date of travel, starting point, destination, purpose of the trip, and total miles traveled.

·       Receipts for any tolls or parking fees incurred during the trip must be attached to the form.

5. Approval Process

All mileage reimbursement forms must be reviewed and approved by the employee's immediate supervisor or manager. Forms with incomplete or inaccurate information will be returned to the employee for correction.

6. Payment

Reimbursement payments will be processed within 30 days of the approved submission. Payments will be made via direct deposit or added to the employee's regular paycheck.

7. Compliance

Employees are expected to comply with this policy and provide accurate information. Any fraudulent claims will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

8. Policy Review

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure compliance with current laws and regulations.

Please contact Human Resources with any questions or concerns regarding this policy.
